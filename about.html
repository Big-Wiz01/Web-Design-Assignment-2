Our agency is committed to building the future of Sierra Leonean football by scouting, nurturing, and promoting young talents with potential to rise on the international stage. We work closely with clubs, coaches, and federations to create pathways for players that go beyond local expectations, opening doors to opportunities across Africa, Europe, Asia and beyond. Our goal is not only to elevate individual players but also to position Sierra Leone as a respected contributor to the global football talent pipeline. With structured development programs, mental and physical performance support, and transparent career management, we ensure our athletes receive the best representation and the strongest foundation to succeed.

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, Elite Football Agency Sierra Leone began as a small initiative to support local players seeking professional advancement. Over the years, the agency has grown into a recognized institution in talent development and international placement. From our first player signing in 2021 to multiple overseas trials by 2024, our journey reflects our commitment to excellence, integrity, and opportunity creation.
